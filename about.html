About me

I was born in 1978, in Liepaja, a city on the shores of the Baltic Sea. I have been drawing since childhood and I always dreamed of painting, but ended up studying economics at Riga Technical University.

Since 1998 I have been working in the advertising and marketing industry, successfully developing many Latvian products and brands. . I am always looking for creative non-standard solutions in my work.

The turning point in my creative life was 2010, when I started studying classical drawing and painting. I gained the foundations for artistic mastery, and I gained knowledge about the world of classical realism art.

Art studies

Within the framework of the ERASMUS program, I got the chance to study at the Krakow Academy of Arts in 2017.

I graduated from the Latvian Academy of Arts and obtained a bachelor’s degree in the Department of Visual. Arts, Painting in 2018.

I was also awarded multiple times for my works, the most significant were winning the Latvian State Forest Scholarship in Art (2017) and the Environmental Objects Festival, where my “Christmas Tree Road” Object AURORA has received the main prize of the festival.

My inspiration

‘Many characters and stories have lived in my head for many years and are now gradually appearing on the canvas one after another.’

I admire the beauty of both nature and the most ordinary things, and I try to display my emotions on canvas in landscapes and still lives.

I am fascinated by people’s faces, movements and characters, so I enjoy portraits and figurative compositions with pleasure.
